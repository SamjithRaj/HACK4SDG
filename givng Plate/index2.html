Welcome to The Giving Plate

I Want to Donate I Need Food

Our Mission

We connect restaurants and event organizers with nearby orphanages and old age homes to ensure that no good food goes to waste and no one goes hungry.

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up as a food donor or receiver

2. Log Food

Donors list available food, receivers post needs

3. Connect

We match donors with nearby receivers

4. Deliver

Food is delivered to those who need it most
